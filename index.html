About Me

Hi there, my name is Aaron Keisler. I've always had a interest in computers and coding, but only recently began to take strong steps towards furthering my web development skillset. I've been focusing on Full Stack Javascript and the MERN (Mongo, Express, React, and Node) Stack. I also have some experience with Python and Visual Basic in Excel for some basic automation and scripting. To further add to my skillset, I am currently taking the Part-time Web Development Bootcamp through George Washington University.

Alongside this course, I've been working my way through Wes Bos's Javascript30, ES6 for Beginners, and React for Beginners courses. I've combined this with a commitment to 100 Days of Code, though I've only been logging locally for now.

My professional background is in Legal Technology and eDiscovery. Over 6 years, I have gained experience with services such as Relativity (v8.2, v9.6, v9.7), One-O, Servient, Concordance, and others and enjoy employing various tactics to complete cleaning, parsing, and searching projects with produced data.

Please contact me via the form on this page (not yet operational) or my email if you have any questions or wish to discuss any potential projects.
